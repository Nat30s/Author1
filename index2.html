Confessions of a Dangerous Girl

Disguised as a psychopath as a child and trained as an assassin by the CIA, Emma Camberright is no ordinary teenager. But now Emma faces the one mission she isn't prepared for—making friends in an ordinary high school.

Meet the Author

I fell in love with books at a young age, and though I studied English literature at the University of Notre Dame, I always maintained my fascination with young adult novels, especially fantasy and adventure stories. After teaching high school English in Brooklyn, New York, and Texas, I went to law school and entered private practice in Chicago, where I occasionally also teach legal writing and write articles about constitutional law and legal history. Confessions of a Dangerous Girl is my first novel.
